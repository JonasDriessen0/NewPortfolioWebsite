Linked

A short platformer game jam game

About the Project

This project was made within a week for the Goeware Limited Color palette game jam. I went into this jam knowing that I wanted to make a simple but fluid third person platformer game because I've always wanted to make one of those games and I thought that this was going to be the perfect opportunity for it. I also deliberately chose a simplistic white and blue color palette since we only had 8 colors to pick from in this jam and I thought that white and blue work nicely together, and turns out they do!

Player Controller

I wanted to create a player controller that was easy to understand but also a bit challenging at the same time so I could add a little bit of challenge to the game. The movement system focuses on momentum and precision, requiring players to carefully plan their jumps and movements through the interconnected platforms.

Game Design & Mechanics

The core mechanic revolves around the connection between different platforms and the player's ability to navigate them smoothly. Each level was designed to test different aspects of the movement system, from basic jumping to more complex maneuvers that combine multiple movement techniques.

Outcome

The game was successfully completed within the jam timeframe and received positive feedback for its clean visual design and tight controls. This project helped me better understand the importance of player feedback in platformer games and how subtle design choices can greatly impact the overall game feel. The limited color palette constraint proved to be more of a creative catalyst than a limitation.
