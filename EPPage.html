A Top Down 2.5D Wave Shooter

Gameplay

EvilPunk is a top-down 2.5D shooter where players use different guns to battle increasing waves of enemies, which become progressively harder.

Enemy Types

Walker

A basic, slow enemy that attacks you when you are close to it.

Slasher

A big enemy with lots of HP that can be dangerous once it gets close to you.

Bomber

A fast enemy with low HP that explodes when it gets close enough to you.

Enemy art by Felishaus Visit their ArtStation

Guns

Clockwork Pistol

A standard handgun with moderate damage and accuracy.

Steam Shotgun

A close-range weapon that fires a spread of bullets.

Automatic Rifle

A fast-firing, fully automatic rifle that is sure to shred your enemy.

Arc Launcher

A weapon that shoots a ball of energy dealing massive damage.

Weapon art by Nou Maaijen. Visit their ArtStation

Gun Selector Functionality

The Gun Selector allows players to switch between weapons during gameplay. This feature is essential as it lets players utilize the various guns in the game.
